# Sending Application via Email

## Subject Line

- Keep it concise and professional: "Application for [Position] - [Your Name]"
- Avoid generic subjects like "Job Application" to increase open rates

## Email Structure

### Greeting

- Use "Dear [Hiring Manager's Name]," if known
- If unknown, use "Dear Hiring Team," or "Dear Sir/Madam,"

### Introduction

- Introduce yourself briefly
- Mention the position and how you found it
- State your interest clearly

### Body

- Highlight relevant skills and experience (2-3 paragraphs)
- Reference specific qualifications from the job description
- Include achievements and examples
- Explain why you're interested in the company and role

### Closing

- Express enthusiasm for an interview
- Thank the recipient for their time
- Provide contact information
- Sign off professionally: "Best regards," or "Sincerely,"

### Attachments

- Attach your resume as PDF: "Resume\_[Your_Name].pdf"
- Include a cover letter if appropriate
- Ensure file names are professional

## Best Practices

- Research the company and personalize the email
- Proofread for grammar and spelling errors
- Keep the email concise (under 250 words)
- Use a professional email address
- Send from an email associated with your name
- Follow up if you don't hear back after 1-2 weeks

## Sample Email Template

```
Subject: Application for Software Developer Position - John Doe

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the Software Developer position advertised on LinkedIn. With 3 years of experience in full-stack development, I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to [Company Name]'s innovative projects.

In my current role at XYZ Corp, I have successfully developed and maintained web applications using React and Node.js, improving user engagement by 25%. I am proficient in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and have experience with Agile methodologies.

I am particularly drawn to [Company Name] because of your commitment to [something specific about the company]. I believe my skills in [relevant skills] align well with the requirements of this role.

I have attached my resume for your review. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background and skills can benefit your team. Thank you for considering my application.

Best regards,
John Doe
Phone: (123) 456-7890
Email: john.doe@email.com
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Don't copy-paste generic content
- Avoid sending mass emails
- Don't forget to include contact information
- Ensure formatting is clean in plain text emails
